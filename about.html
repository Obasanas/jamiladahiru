About Dr. Jamila

Academic Biography

Jamila Mohammed Dahiru currently works at the Department of Mass Communication, Ahmadu Bello University. Jamila does research in Qualitative Social Research, Quantitative Social Research and Communication and Media. Their current project is 'Knowledge and Utilization of health informatics among the medical doctors in Ahmadu Bello University Teaching Hospital, Zaria'.

A committed educator, strategic communicator, and public servant, Dr. Dahiru's professional journey spanning over 15 years across academia, government, and the development sector has equipped her with a unique blend of scholarly depth, policy insight, and practical leadership in some of Nigeria's most pressing social challenges.

Her academic foundation is built on a Ph.D. in Mass Communication from Bayero University Kano, where she focused on the intersections of media, education, and social change. As a Lecturer in Strategic Communication at the University of Abuja, and formerly at Ahmadu Bello University Zaria, she has taught and mentored hundreds of students in courses ranging from development communication to media ethics, health communication, and marketing.

Beyond academia, Dr. Dahiru has been privileged to serve Nigeria through public office — serving two terms as Commissioner for Education in Bauchi State and chairing the State Steering Committee for the World Bank-supported AGILE Project for adolescent girls' education and empowerment.
