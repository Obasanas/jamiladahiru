Social Impact

WeCare Centre for Community Awareness & Empowerment Initiative

Founded by Dr. Dahiru, WeCare is a nonprofit operating across Northern Nigeria, supporting vulnerable children, promoting menstrual hygiene education, running vocational trainings for young women, and delivering public health interventions to Almajiri schools.

Through community outreach and grassroots partnerships, WeCare has empowered hundreds of girls and families to reclaim their right to dignity, education, and opportunity.
